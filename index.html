Product

The Best Product

Fresh Young Coconut

Young coconut is the name for coconut (Cocos nucifera) with green trunk. Young coconut water is very refreshing and has many benefits for our body. The flesh of the fruit is not as thick as old coconut which is usually used for coconut milk, young coconut can be consumed raw and the texture of young coconut meat is very soft and delicious. We only sell the best quality of fresh young coconuts, the weight of fresh young coconuts is about 0.5 – 1.2 kg per piece (can be selected according to customer requests).

Fresh Mature Coconut

Unlike young coconut, mature coconut has brown shell. Mature coconut water does not contain as much sugar as young coconut water but the flesh mature coconut is thicker. One of the benefits of the water of mature coconut is to prevent diabetes. Mature coconut meat can be cooked or used as coconut oil. The maturer the coconut, the higher the fat content. Usually, this mature coconut is bought to be processed into copra and coconut oil. We only sell the best quality of fresh mature coconuts, the weight of fresh mature coconuts is about 0.5 – 1.2 kg per piece (can be selected according to customer requests).

Semi-Husked Coconut

Semi-husked coconut is a fully matured coconuts but while de-husking, the husk is left intact over the “eyes” of the coconut. The “eyes” of the coconut is a sensitive area, and by protecting it we can increase the shelf life of the coconut as the area susceptible to decay is protected.

Cocofiber

Cocofiber is a fiber from coconut husks in dry conditions. From cocofibre products will produce various kinds of product derivations whose benefits are extraordinary. In several countries, including Indonesia, coconut husk is processed into plant fertilizer.

Robusta Coffee Bean

Robusta is a type of coffee plant with the scientific name Coffea canephora. As the name implies, this drink extracted from Robusta coffee beans has a strong taste and tends to be more bitter than Arabica. We sell them in the form of green beans and will package them according to customer requests.
